Library Access & Hours

calendar_today Last updated: Oct 12, 2024
schedule 4 min read
Qianhu Campus

The Qianhu Campus Library is the intellectual heart of Nanchang University. Spanning seven floors, it houses over 2 million volumes and provides diverse study environments ranging from collaborative zones to silent reading rooms. Access is strictly controlled via student ID cards, so ensuring your card is active is the first step to utilizing this resource.

The central atrium of the main library.

Opening Hours

The library operates on a seasonal schedule that adjusts slightly during exam weeks and public holidays. Generally, the main circulation desk and study areas follow these hours:

  • Weekdays (Mon-Fri): 07:00 AM – 10:30 PM
  • Weekends (Sat-Sun): 08:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • Public Holidays: 09:00 AM – 05:00 PM (Subject to notice)
info

Entry Requirement

You must have your physical Campus Card to enter the gates. Using the electronic QR code via the NCU app is currently only supported at the North Gate entrance.

Study Room Reservations

During midterms and finals, finding a seat can be competitive. The university utilizes a WeChat mini-program called "NCU Library Seat" (南昌大学图书馆座位) to manage high-demand areas.

  1. Search for "NCU Library" in WeChat.
  2. Navigate to Service > Seat Reservation.
  3. Login with your Student ID and the last 6 digits of your ID card.
  4. Select a floor, zone, and specific seat number.

Once reserved, you have 20 minutes to "check-in" at the library entrance kiosks. Failure to check in three times in a semester will result in a 7-day ban from the reservation system.

Wi-Fi Access

The library is covered by the NCU-WLAN network. For optimal speed, it is recommended to use the 5GHz frequency band.

Network SSID
NCU-WLAN-5G
